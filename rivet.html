Riveting Courses on Battery Management and EV

  • By Finny Philip Biju
  • May 23, 2020

Advanced Systems Methodologies for EV 10 day training by Devise Electronics. Pvt Ltd

This was a 10 day live training course which covered the overall working of EV, software like Ricardo used for vehicle simulation and BMS utility software. The course also covered modern technologies like Autonomous vehicles and advanced vehicle communication systems like VCU, sensors etc.

Through this course I learned how to design an EV, which include selection of various Electronic systems, components, powertrain and design considerations.

Finny Philip Biju

Introduction to Battery Management System (offered by University of Colorado Boulder)

From this 4 week intermediate course, I have learned the working principles of Li-ion cells,BMS and the design requirements. The course covered the functions of BMS in-depth and basic calculation of cell soc, soh etc. BMS sensing, high voltage control, protection of batteries, diagnostics and interfacing with VCU was explained in detail. Through this course I was able to develop a basic understanding of BMS, Li-ion battery overall and what all further things I require for implementing it successfully in EV

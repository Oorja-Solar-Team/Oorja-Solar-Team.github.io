My Experience in taking the Devise Electronics EV Course

  • By Akshay P R
  • May 29, 2020

EV_Technology Course offered by Devise Electronics. Pvt Ltd

Recently I attended a 10 day online course conducted by Devise Electronics Pvt Ltd. The course is certified ASME course and hence the certificate provided has great value.The entire team of faculty are industrial experts who have years of experience.The teams linkedin and online profile speaks for their expertise .The company provides online training every 2 weeks specializing in technologies related to Electric vehicle.
The course I attended was intended on helping us to teach basics of how to build a electric vehicle.The course was very useful and worth every penny in my opinion, before attending the course the part I had problem with calculating the power requirement of the motor.The first day of the course was all about Range and Power calculations of the motor which was exactly the part I wanted to learn.Day 2 delved into the details of motor,The types of motors, the efficiency curve,how to control the characteristics of the motor using the inverters and speed controls.Day 3 was about driveline configuration which is about how motors can and are installed in vehicles.Day 4 was about different battery technologies and industurial methods such as battery swapping etc. This days course was especially good as they even gave different manufacturers that are available in the market.Day 5 and the last day of first week of the course was about BMS and BMS software the faculty took the effort to teach us about BMS by actually using a software this according to another member of the Oorja who is in charge of BMS tells me was very useful for him.Day 6 and the first day of second week introduced us to Ricardo ignite simulation software that is used by many industry OEMs to test and simulate their products before manufacturing.Day 7 talked about charging technologies available in the market such as CHademo,CCS etc.This was a useful session as it made us realize that direct charging by the solar panel and discharging by the motor will not be simultaneously possible.I also got a surprise when I looked at the mail inbox I found a coupon of 500 rupees which I can use if I apply for another course with them.Day 8 and present day was about protection systems wiring technologies etc used in a electric vehicle to provide isolation and protection

The course I attended was intended on helping us to teach basics of how to build a electric vehicle.The course was very useful and worth every penny in my opinion

I have 2 more days of course left to attend and I am looking forward to them.Some of the positives I found about this course was unlike most courses which are recorded sessions this course is all live webinars where they provide you with time and encourages you to ask doubts.They also started a whatsapp group to help us ask any further doubts I may have,they also take the effort to answer any further doubts I ask them personally.Another great and awesome thing about the course is all webinars are recorded and stored in Wistia which will be available for us through out the lifetime with our registered Email ID this is a very useful feature as it will be available for us throughout our project development phase.They already have plans for next course which will start from June 6 it will be about Ricardo Ignite simulation software they tellme it will only cost below a thousand rupees so I know now where I am going to use my 500 rupees coupon.Hope I will meet you all at the course .

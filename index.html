About Us

The Oorja Solar Car team is an independent, student-led organization that operates on the campus of Model Engineering College Kochi, Kerala , India. With a large and active student body consisting of students from various colleges. Which comes together and works during the college year to design and construct efficient and self sustaining solar vehicles, which will be used in STEM outreach.

The team mainly focus on development of cleaner and efficient mobile vehicle . and there by compete in national solar car competitions which includes the very distinguished Surya Kranti Solar Car Race India. The main vision of the team is to bring up an awareness and develop sustainable form of energy which can be easily feasible for the daily denizens making solar energy the get to go source of energy .
